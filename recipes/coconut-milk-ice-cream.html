Coconut Milk Ice Cream

All information here is from this website

Description

"This coconut milk ice cream, lightly flavored with vanilla, is a delicious dairy-free treat."

Ingredients

  • 1 (13.5 ounce) can unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Whisk together coconut milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt in a small bowl until sugar dissolves
  2. Chill until cold, at least 2 hours. Churn in an ice cream maker according to manufacturerâ€™s directions.
  3. Freeze in an airtight container at least 4 hours or up to 3 months.
